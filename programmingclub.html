The vision and activities of this club is not limited only to the traditional C/C++, Java but dwells out to a broader area of developing the algorithmic skills. Towards accomplishing our aim the club conducts relevant lectures, workshops, seminars and weekly online programming contests. The club provides the students with solid algorithmic and programming foundation as such that will help them in their placements and in different programming contests. With the help of various faculties the club conducts sessions with relevant companies and creating such projects that amply familiarizes the students with creating large and commercial softwares.
