Rules

  1. Only students from the colleges are eligible for prizes.

  2. This is a individual participation hackathon, you have participate individually. No Team participation.

  3. You have to develop using only C/C++. Any other language is not supported for this event.

  4. The hackathon will start at 13-11-2016 10:00 AM and end at 13-11-2016 1:00 PM, you work on your hack during the allotted time only.

  5. It's an online hackathon, you can participate from anywhere.

  6. Themes of the hackathon will be published when the event will get started in Hackathon Page at KodeCracker website(https://www.kodecracker.com).

  7. You are expected to come up with new and innovative ideas, any idea that has been copied from somewhere will be disqualified.

  8. Your hack must be developed entirely during the Hackathon duration.

  9. The intellectual property of your code belongs only to you.

  10. Register yourself in Kodecracker website(https://www.kodecracker.com). You will find the Hackathon Page in Arena Section.

  11. The file size should not exceed more than 50 MB. You have to share Google Drive files with hackathon host coordinator e-mail which you will find in the Hackathon Page at KodeCracker.

  12. No, one does not need to be logged in or be online for the entire duration. You can develop the application on your local system based on the given themes and then upload it to Google Drive.

  13. You have to develop the entire software application on your local system and submit it before the time ends in zip/rar/tar.gz file format along with instructions to run the application and source code.

  14. The detail instruction for sharing the file alongwith themes will be posted in Hackathon Page at KodeCracker when the event will get start.
